About Us

Packaging solutions built on quality, care, and continuous improvement.

Our teams deliver reliable products and services while staying true to our values.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of food and industrial packaging products.

Our Mission

We are committed to achieve our vision through customers by delivering high quality and competitive products and services, through functional excellence by continuously developing and improving our processes and resources, and through employees by maintaining a high performance and high efficiency organization aligned with our core values.

Our Values

Our values are malasakit, being customer centric, teamwork, excellence, commitment, honesty and integrity, discipline, and principles guided by God.
